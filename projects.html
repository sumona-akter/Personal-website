Projects

Invasive Bacterial Disease Surveillance

This project focuses on monitoring and characterizing invasive bacterial pathogens to understand their epidemiology, resistance patterns, and genomic variations, with an emphasis on public health interventions.

Epidemiology of Neonatal Candidemia

A study investigating the prevalence, risk factors, and resistance mechanisms of Candidemia in neonates in resource-limited settings to guide better treatment protocols.

Molecular Assessment of Thoracic Empyema in Asian Children

This collaborative study aimed to identify and characterize the bacterial and viral pathogens associated with thoracic empyema in children across Asia using advanced molecular and genomic tools.
